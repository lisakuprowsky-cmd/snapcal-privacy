# Privacy Policy for SnapCal: Food & Calorie Log

Last updated: June 9, 2026

SnapCal: Food & Calorie Log (“SnapCal,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SnapCal mobile app.

## Information We Collect

SnapCal may collect or process information you choose to provide in the app, including food logs, calorie entries, meal photos, barcode scans, weight or measurement entries, and app usage information.

If you use photo-based food scanning, meal images may be processed to estimate food items and calories. Calorie and nutrition results are estimates and should not be considered medical advice.

## How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features such as calorie tracking, AI food scanning, barcode scanning, meal history, progress tracking, and subscription access.

## Subscriptions

SnapCal offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple’s In-App Purchase system. Purchases and billing are handled by Apple. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

## Third-Party Services

SnapCal may use third-party services to support app functionality, analytics, storage, or AI-powered food recognition. These services may process limited information necessary to provide the app’s features.

## Data Retention

We retain information only as needed to provide SnapCal’s features, improve the app, comply with legal obligations, or resolve issues.

## Children’s Privacy

SnapCal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Health Disclaimer

SnapCal provides calorie and nutrition estimates for general wellness and informational purposes only. SnapCal is not a medical device and does not provide medical, dietary, or health advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health or diet decisions.
